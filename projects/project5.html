Project 5: Spirograph/plotter

About

Our fifth project challenged us to build a spirograph, or spinning art machine. I decided I wanted more of a challenge, so I tried to design a robot that could write or print any set of commands. I modeled my design off of a 3-d printer, but meant to draw in 2d. I later learned this type of machine is called a plotter. I decided to keep the rotational functionality so it was also technically a spirograph. Although I ran out of time to improve my design or program something more interesting for it to draw, I'm happy that it succeeded in writing my first initial.

Videos

Conclusion

My design ended up a little rough around the edges, but I'd be excited to take on this challenge again and improve my method!
